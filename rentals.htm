Welcome to our recycle center's product page! We are proud to offer a wide variety of environmentally-friendly products that will help you reduce your carbon footprint and make a positive impact on the planet. Here are some of our most popular products.

What Can Be Recycled

1.) Plastic: bottles, jugs, containers labeled

Paper and cardboard: newspapers, magazines, office paper, cardboard boxes

2.) Glass: bottles and jars (clear, brown, green)

Metal: aluminum cans, steel cans, tin cans

What Can't Be Recycled

1.) Plastic bags and film

Clothing and textiles

2.) Styrofoam and packing peanuts

Food waste and compostable materials

2019 Recycle Terms

Terms of Service: At our recycling center, we are committed to promoting sustainability and protecting the environment. In order to ensure the safety and efficiency of our operations, we ask that all customers follow our rules and guidelines. These include sorting your recyclables properly, not bringing hazardous materials, following all posted signs and instructions, and respecting our staff and other customers. By using our recycling center, you agree to abide by these rules and help us create a cleaner, greener future.
