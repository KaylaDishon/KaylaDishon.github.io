Reduce. Reuse. Recycle.

At Walker's Recycle Center, we believe that every little bit counts when it comes to protecting the environment. That's why we provide a convenient and eco-friendly way to dispose of your waste through recycling.

We accept a variety of materials for recycling, including:

  • Paper: including newspapers, magazines, cardboard, and office paper.
  • Metals: such as aluminum cans, tin cans, and steel cans.
  • Glass: including glass bottles and jars.
  • Organic waste - including food scraps, yard waste, and compostable materials
  • Plastics: such as water bottles, milk jugs, shampoo bottles, and food containers.
  • Batteries: including household batteries and car batteries.
  • Textiles: such as clothing and linens.

We also offer pickup services for businesses and organizations looking to recycle large volumes of materials. Our team is committed to providing efficient and reliable service, and we work with our clients to develop customized recycling solutions tailored to their specific needs.

Visit Us Today

If you have any questions about our recycling services or would like to schedule a pickup, please don't hesitate to contact us or visit our center. Our friendly and knowledgeable staff are always happy to assist you and help you find the best recycling solution for your needs.
